Installing the Kernel

Contents
  • Just one step (as root): 
    	# make install
    On many distros (e.g. RHEL5, RHEL6, SLES 11) that's it.

  • Ubuntu requires additional steps: 
    	# update-initramfs -c -k kernel-version
	# update-grub
So, what goes on behind the scenes of "make install"?
